# PortfolioOS Extension — Store Listing Copy

## Name (≤45 chars)
PortfolioOS — EPF & PPF Auto-Sync

## Short description (≤132 chars)
Auto-sync your EPF passbook and PPF balances to PortfolioOS while you browse netbanking. Encrypted, private, India-focused.

## Detailed description
PortfolioOS Auto-Sync is a companion to your PortfolioOS web account that keeps your EPF and PPF balances current without copy-paste.

### How it works
1. Pair the extension with your PortfolioOS account using a one-time code (5-minute TTL).
2. Log in to your EPFO or supported bank netbanking site as you normally would.
3. The extension reads only the passbook / statement table on those pages and securely sends the structured data to your PortfolioOS account.
4. Your dashboard shows the latest balances and transactions — no manual entry.

### Supported sites
- EPFO Member Passbook (passbook.epfindia.gov.in)
- EPFO Unified Portal (unifiedportal-mem.epfindia.gov.in)
- State Bank of India netbanking (onlinesbi.sbi, retail.onlinesbi.sbi)

More banks (HDFC, ICICI, Axis, PNB, Bank of Baroda, India Post) coming soon.

### Privacy first
- The extension only activates on the listed financial sites — it cannot read any other page.
- It never reads your password, OTP, or any credentials.
- Your PortfolioOS bearer token is stored in your browser's encrypted storage and can be revoked from the popup at any time.
- Full privacy policy: https://portfolio-os.up.railway.app/privacy

### What you need
- An active PortfolioOS account (sign up at https://portfolio-os.up.railway.app)
- Chrome 120+ or compatible Chromium browser
